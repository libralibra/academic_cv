About Me

An accomplished Senior Lecturer and module leader with expertise in Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (AI/ML) and Computational Engineering; Fellow of the Higher Education Academy (FHEA); Winner of Rolls-Royce Defence Pioneering Digital Award and Rolls-Royce CFD Review Meeting Best Poster Award; Experienced senior researcher served Rolls-Royce UTC at the University of Southampton over a decade; The creator of 10+ successful software packages and platforms for Rolls-Royce; Innovative solutions and consultancy services provider collaborated with industrial clients such as Airbus, the Royal Air Force, the UK Ministry of Defence (MoD), Defence Science and Technology Laboratory (DSTL), UK Aerospace Technology Institute (ATI), etc.
