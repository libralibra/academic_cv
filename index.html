Profile

An accomplished Senior Lecturer with expertise in Computational Engineering and Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (AI/ML); Module leaders of two MSc courses in Classical Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning; Experienced Senior Researcher served Rolls-Royce UTC at the University of Southampton over a decade; The creator of about 10 successful software packages and platforms for Rolls-Royce; Winner of Rolls-Royce Defence Pioneering Digital Award in 2020; Innovative industrial solutions and consultancy services provider for clients such as Airbus, the Royal Air Force, the UK Ministry of Defence (MoD), Defence Science and Technology Laboratory (DSTL), UK Aerospace Technology Institute (ATI), etc.
